<h1>Season Subalias<img align="right" src="../../../Data/images/main.png" width="100px"></h1>

Removal of a season of the specified location (requires Dragonspeaker)

## Usage
`!weather removal season [location name] [season name]`
- `location name`
    - Required
    - Name of location
- `season name`
    - Required
    - Name of season
